Frequently Asked Questions

What is Vastrayl?

Vastrayl is a fashion-first social platform where users showcase their daily outfits, rate looks using a transparent 0–10 scale, and participate in themed style contests. It’s built for people who see fashion as a form of self-expression.

How is Vastrayl different from Instagram?

Unlike traditional social media platforms, Vastrayl focuses on fashion discovery rather than follower counts or likes. Outfits are rated based on creativity, styling, and overall vibe using a fair and visible rating system.

Is Vastrayl only for people who want to participate in style contests?

Not at all. Style contests are completely optional. You can upload your daily outfits, explore looks from others, and build your personal style profile without entering any contest.

Vastrayl works both as a fashion discovery platform and a competitive space — you choose how you want to express your style.

What types of style contests are available on Vastrayl?

Vastrayl hosts a wide variety of themed contests designed for everyone. These include office wear, gym outfits, street style, ethnic looks, minimal fashion, experimental fits, and many more.

Whether you’re a working professional, a student, a fitness enthusiast, or someone who loves experimenting with fashion, there’s a contest for every lifestyle and aesthetic.

How does the rating system work?

Instead of likes, Vastrayl uses a 0–10 rating scale. This encourages honest feedback and helps highlight genuinely creative and well-styled outfits within the community.

Is AI-generated content allowed on Vastrayl?

Vastrayl is built around authentic fashion and real self-expression. To maintain fairness, users can flag content they believe is AI-generated. Flagged content may be reviewed to ensure transparency within the community.

At the same time, Vastrayl recognizes creativity in all forms. Dedicated contests may be hosted specifically for AI-generated or digitally created fashion concepts, clearly labeled so users know what they are viewing and rating.

This approach allows real-world fashion and creative experimentation to coexist while preserving trust and originality on the platform.

Who can use Vastrayl?

Vastrayl is built for Gen-Z, college students, influencers, fashion creators, working professionals, and anyone who enjoys expressing themselves through style.

Is Vastrayl available globally?

Yes. Vastrayl is designed as a global platform. Users from around the world can upload outfits, discover trends, and participate in style contests.

Some contests may be region-specific to highlight local fashion, while others are open globally to celebrate diverse styles and cultures.

Is Vastrayl free to use?

Yes. Vastrayl is free to download and use. Users can upload outfits, rate looks, and join style contests without any cost.

How can creators grow on Vastrayl?

Creators grow by consistently sharing outfits, receiving high ratings, participating in contests, and engaging with the community — not through paid reach or artificial boosts.

Is my data safe on Vastrayl?

Yes. Vastrayl uses secure authentication and follows industry-standard practices to protect user data and privacy.
